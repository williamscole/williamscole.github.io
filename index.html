About

CW
Postdoctoral Researcher · UCSF

I'm Cole Williams, a postdoctoral researcher in Dr. Elad Ziv's lab at UCSF.

I recently completed my PhD in Dr. Sohini Ramachandran's group at Brown University, where I was supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. My thesis was titled Population-Genetic Methods and Insights Across the Spectrum of Relatedness.

My work develops statistical and computational methods in population genetics, with a focus on identity-by-descent, relatedness inference, and genetic variation across diverse populations.
